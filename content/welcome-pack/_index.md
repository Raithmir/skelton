# Welcome to Skelton Gate!

We're delighted to welcome you to the Skelton Gate community. This guide will help you settle into your new home and discover everything our estate has to offer.

## Getting Started

### Your First Week

When you first move in, here are the essential tasks to complete:

1. **Register with the Management Company** - Ensure you're on record as the property owner/tenant
2. **Set Up Utilities** - Arrange connections for gas, electricity, water, and broadband
3. **Register for Council Tax** - Contact Leeds City Council to set up payments
4. **Update Your Address** - Notify banks, employers, DVLA, and other important contacts
5. **Meet Your Neighbours** - Introduce yourself and join the community

### Property Information

- **Builder:** Check your property documents for warranty information
- **Snagging Period:** Most builders offer a 2-year warranty period for defects
- **Home Emergency Cover:** Consider arranging cover for boiler, plumbing, and electrical issues
- **Building Insurance:** Usually included in service charges for apartments; homeowners should arrange their own

## Utilities Setup

### Electricity & Gas

Your property may already have meters installed. You'll need to:

1. Take opening meter readings on your move-in date
2. Choose an energy supplier (or stick with the current one)
3. Set up a direct debit or payment plan
4. Consider fixed-rate tariffs to avoid price fluctuations

### Water

Yorkshire Water supplies all properties in the area:

- No need to choose a supplier
- Set up your account online or by phone
- Water meters are installed in all properties
- Payment plans available

### Broadband

Skelton Gate has access to full-fibre broadband (FTTP):

- Check our [Broadband Providers](/broadband) page for options
- Multiple providers offer gigabit speeds
- Installation typically takes 1-2 weeks
- Consider booking before you move in

### TV & Phone

- **Freeview:** Available via aerial (check TV signal coverage)
- **Sky/Virgin Media:** Available but may require installation
- **Landline:** Optional - most residents use mobile phones only
